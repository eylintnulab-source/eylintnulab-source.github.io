李祐慈老師計算化學實驗室

Elise Y. Li's Computational Chemistry Research Group applies first-principles calculations and theoretical chemistry to understand materials, catalytic reactions, electrochemistry, and molecular design.

Explore Research

Research

We study solid-state materials and discrete molecular systems with computational chemistry, connecting microscopic mechanisms to design principles for experiments.

First-principles materials chemistry

Structural, electronic, magnetic, and luminescence properties of periodic and molecular systems.

Catalysis and electrochemistry

Reaction mechanisms for catalytic and electrochemical systems, including fuel cells and CO2 recycling.

Energy and nano materials

Computational design for energy storage, nanomaterials, carbon-based systems, and molecular spintronics.
